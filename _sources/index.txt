Welcome to the documentation for Erebot_Module_Roulette!
========================================================

Erebot_Module_Roulette is a module for `Erebot`_ that provides a simple
Russian Roulette game.

For example:

..  sourcecode:: irc

    15:34:45 < Foo> !roulette
    15:34:45 < Erebot> Foo: chamber 1 of 6 => +click+
    15:43:05 < Bar> !roulette
    15:43:06 < Erebot> Bar: chamber 2 of 6 => *BANG*
    15:43:06 * Erebot reloads
